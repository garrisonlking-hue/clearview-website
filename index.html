FAQ

Questions, answered.

How much does window cleaning cost?
Most single-family homes fall between $200 and $450 depending on the number of windows, stories, and how long it's been since the last cleaning. Use the estimator above for a quick range — we confirm the exact price at a free in-person walkthrough.
What is pure-water cleaning?
We push 100% deionized purified water through a soft-bristle brush on a telescoping pole. No detergents, no ladders against your siding, and no streaks — pure water dries completely spot-free and keeps your glass cleaner longer.
Do you clean interior windows too?
Yes. We hand-clean interior glass with a streak-free solution and detail your sills and tracks, while exteriors are cleaned with our pure-water system. Choose interior, exterior, or both when you request a quote.
Are you insured?
Absolutely. Clear View Window Co. is fully insured, so your home and property are protected on every job.
Do you offer pressure washing?
Yes — we pressure wash siding, decks, patios, and walkways to clear dirt, mildew, and grime and instantly lift your home's curb appeal. Add it to any window cleaning visit.
What towns do you serve?
Columbia, Coventry, Mansfield, Andover, Tolland, Storrs, Manchester, Glastonbury, Vernon, Bolton and the surrounding Eastern Connecticut area. Call us if you don't see yours.
