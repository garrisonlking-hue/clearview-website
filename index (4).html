How We Clean

Pure water. Spotless results.

We use professional water-fed pole technology — the gold standard in modern window cleaning — paired with careful hand-work on the inside.

Professional Equipment

The Water-Fed Pole System

We use 100% deionized purified water pushed through a soft-bristle brush on a telescoping pole. No detergents. No ladders leaning on your siding. No streaks left behind.

✓
Safer for your home — we clean from the ground, no ladders pressed against your gutters or siding.
✓
Zero chemicals — safe for plants, pets, kids, and our environment.
✓
Stays cleaner longer — pure water leaves no detergent residue, so dust and pollen don't stick.
✓
Reaches every window — second-story, dormers, picture windows — all from solid ground.
Our 4-Step Process

Simple, careful, done right.

01

Free Estimate

We visit your home, walk the property with you, and provide an upfront written quote. No pressure, no surprises.

02

Interior Hand-Wash

We hand-clean every inside pane with a streak-free solution. Sills and tracks are detailed. Furniture is protected.

03

Exterior Pure Water

Our water-fed pole system uses purified water to scrub and rinse every exterior window — frame to frame.

What We Offer

Focused on what we do best.

Residential window cleaning, done properly — with the equipment, training, and care your home deserves.

Window Cleaning

Interior and exterior windows hand-cleaned and pure-water rinsed. Streak-free, every time.

Screen Cleaning

Pulled, deep-washed off-frame, dried, and reinstalled. Airflow restored, appearance like new.

Sill & Track Detailing

Built-up dirt, dead bugs, and grime carefully cleaned out of sills and tracks. Often the biggest visible difference.

Hard Water Stain Removal

Mineral stains from sprinklers and rain runoff that regular cleaning can't touch. We restore glass clarity.
